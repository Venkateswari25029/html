Amrita University,Delhi

located in Faridabad, is a major health sciences and research hub featuring India's largest multi-specialty hospital
Location & Focus: Situated in Faridabad, the campus acts as a major center for medical education and research, serving as the 8th campus of Amrita University.
            Academic Programs:
            Engineering: B.Tech and M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science.
            Health Sciences: Medicine (MBBS), Nursing, Public Health, Hospital Administration, and Allied Health Sciences.
            Research: The Amrita Research Center (ARCD) focuses on biomedical innovation, cell biology, genomics, and regenerative medicine.
